Ingredients

    Basic Sandwich Ingredients
  • Bread
  • Protein(chicken, sliced turkey, tuna, egg or cheese)
  • Vegetables(lettuce, tomato slices, onion, pickles, etc)
    Condiments (Optional)
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Butter
  • Hummus
  • Pesto

Steps

  1. Wash and slices vegetables
  2. Toast the bread (optional)
  3. Spread the condiments
  4. Layer the ingredients
  5. Close and Slice
  6. Serve
